Our Products

Munich Helles

A clean, malty, golden colored German lager with a grainy-sweet malt flavor and a smooth, dry finish.

Irish Red Ale

An easy to drink pint, often with subtle flavours. Slightly malty in the balance at times with a mild caramel/toffee sweetness initially, a slightly grainy and biscuity palate, and a touch of toasty dryness on the finish.

Dry Stout

Dry stout is black beer with a dry-roasted character thanks to the use of roasted barley. The emphasis on coffee-like roasted barley and a moderate degree of roasted malt aromas define much of the character.

American Pale Ale

A pale, refreshing and hoppy ale, still with enough malt support to make the beer balanced and drinkable. The clean presence of hops can reflect classic or modern varieties, American or New World, with a wide range of characteristics.

English Porter

A moderate strength red beer with a restrained roast character and bitterness. It can have a range of roasted flavors, generally without burnt qualities, often with a malt-chocolate-caramel profile.

American Black

Hop flavor and aroma are medium-high. Fruity, citrus, piney, floral and herbal character from hops of all origins may contribute to the overall experience. This beer is often called a black IPA or Cascadian dark ale.
